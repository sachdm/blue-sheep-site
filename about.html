About Us

We're a lean crew who believe the best stories are found, not forced — shot on real locations, with real people, at the pace the story actually needs.

Blue Sheep handles development, production, and post for independent features, branded content, and shorts. Founded in 2023, we've kept the team small on purpose — every project gets the same attention, whether it's a 90-second spot or a feature doc.
